What We Do

This initiative is a wing of the DreamSpace Academy aims to support young girls and women in science, technology, engineering, mathematics and arts (STEAM). DoW firmly believes in a world where women are embraced in STEAM fields and are able to equally contribute to the development of a community and a country through those sectors.

Our Vision

To create a world where the dreams of women in STEAM are nurtured and to build a space where women have been equally embraced in all sectors, breaking any stigma against them in the Global South.

Our Mission

To build the STEAM dreams of girls and women through mentoring, challenged-based learning, grassroots innovation, and impact venture building.
